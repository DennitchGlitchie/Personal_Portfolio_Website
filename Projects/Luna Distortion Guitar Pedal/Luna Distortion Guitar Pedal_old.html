“Luna” Production Quality Guitar Pedal Design

This Project is currently in progress:

The Luna Distortion Pedal is a first in a series of self-built and self-designed electric guitar pedals. Loosely based of the Big Muff, I hope to provide a product that will give a loud and lustrous sound to your guitar tone.

The prototype board was created and WORKS! I have included a demo video of me absolutely shredding below:
