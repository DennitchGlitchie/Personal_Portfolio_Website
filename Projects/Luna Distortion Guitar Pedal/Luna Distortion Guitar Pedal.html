Luna Distortion Guitar Pedal

“Luna” Production Quality Guitar Pedal Design

This Project is currently in progress

The Luna Distortion Pedal is a first in a series of self-built and self-designed electric guitar pedals. Loosely based of the Ibanez Tube Screamer and Electro-Harmonix Big Muff, I hope to provide a product that will give a loud and lustrous sound to your guitar tone.

First Prototype Learnings:

  • Messed up the footprint to the toggle switch
  • Power regulation circuit needs to be adjusted for faster turn on time
  • Solder pads need to be bigger
  • Currently tracking down hum and screeching
  • Lowering the “Gain” potentiometer (this might help with the undesirable sounds)
  • Potentiometers are backwards
  • Moving the knobs to be centered in the pedal

Checking the acoustics of the caps:

I tapped the board to very that this board doesn’t go crazy because of the types of capacitors I used.

Tracking down the Hum/Screeching:

One thing I’d like to note that this “Hum” is present on the Electro-Harmonix Big Muff Pedal (which is what this pedal is loosely based off of). This suggests to me that there might be something inherent about this undesirable sound. I would still like to track it down though so that I can better understand what is going on.
